About Chin Ching Touwu Plant：

We have provided our entire facility and latest technologies to Minexco (Vietnam), starting from surveying, mining, washing, classifying to shipping via vessel, each every process has gone through a stringent control system. The high purity Vietnam silica sand that we distribute, also goes through rigid iron stripping and grain sizing before supplying to glass foundry, and construction industries. Finally, by using the above silica sand as the main base material, ground by the pebble millers, which are lined with silex, we are able to produce the high purity silica powder with low iron content.

Main Product：

Vietnam Silica Sand：
Catering to electronics industry, high grade glass, foundry, sand blasting, water filtering, pesticide, golf course, horticulture and landscape architecture, alkaline high strength cement, insulting filler, epoxy resin mortar, architectural uses.
High Grade Quality Silica Powder：
Catering to glaze, fiber glass, refractory, EMC, fine-glass polishing, stain removal agent, and filler industries.

About Chin Ching Chung Hsing Plant：

Starting from selecting the right mine with a high purity of silica, then through the rigid production control, we are able to produce many specs of quality foundry and resin coated sand that have low clay content.

Main Product：

Foundry Sand：
Offer in various grain sizes, It is the base material for making CO2, furan, green sand, and resin coated sand molds.
Suitable usages:steel, stainless steel, iron, copper and aluminum castings.
Resin Coated Sand：
With rigid quality control, it comes in extensive specs for using in making auto, motorcycle parts. It is also used in pipe, steel, stainless steel, copper, aluminum castings and medium or large iron or alloy castings for making cores and outer molds.
