Chin Ching groups was established in September of 1997, built Tunglo Plant for producing silica sand and resin coated sand.

With employees over 500, it has four manufacturing sites : Taiwan, China(Dongguang/Suzhou/Tianjin), Thailand and Vietnam and service covers China, Japan, Korea, Indonessia, Singapore, Vietnam, etc.

Starting from supplier evaluations, sample testing, to product applications, and even to after-sale service, we have always been keeping the closest relation with our customers at all time, because we truly believe －only the customer's satisfaction could continue to create the win-win situation for both of us.
