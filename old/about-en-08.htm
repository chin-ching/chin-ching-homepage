Chin Ching groups is established in September of 1997, built Tunglo Plant for producing silica sand and resin coated sand.

With Capital US$70 million and employee over 2000,it has four manufacturing sites: Taiwan (Taoyuan),Southern China(DongGuang/TongShan) and East China(Wuxi) Global logistics/service covers China, Europe,America, Japan, Korea, India and Vietnam.

ITEQ performs not only a copper clad laminate maker, it also roles with provider of key electronic parts. In pursuing for a supplier of quality products, it never stops investing on people, training and research so to meet customer's need.
 
ITEQ Corporation is devoted in the industry as a professional base material provider for Printed Circuit Board. Performance and Cost effectiveness are key drivers to its products. The strength of self-developing research brings it a frontier of halogen free and high performance material suitable for lead free process among world class producers. It provides total solution for the industry with one stop shop of laminates-rigid, flex, CEM, halogen free, environment friendly material and all lead free process compatible materials.

1977 Established Chin Ching Silica Sand Co., Ltd., built Tunglo Plant for producing silica sand and resin coated sand.
　
1981 Established Chin Ching Mines Co., Ltd., to survey and mine the high purity material for silica sand.
　
1983   Built a silica powder plant in Touwu.
1985 Started to produce phenolic resin. Besides for our resin coated sand usage, also exporting it to overseas clients.
　
1986   Built the air-setting fruan resin and hardening agent lines.
1988 Acquired Kuang Ying Mines Co., Ltd. for producing foundry sand ,glass sand and clay.
　
1990  Established K. L. Chemicals Co., Ltd.
1992 Be the agent of Vietnam Camranh Bay silica sand, and built an imported silica sand and production line in Chin Ching Touwu Plant for supplying high purity silica sand and powder.
　
1994  Completion of building K. L. Chemicals Co., Ltd. Daja Plant.
1995   Built a reclaiming sand production line in Chin Ching Tunglo Plant
1996 K. L. Chemicals Co., Ltd. successfully developed the phenolic resin for bakelite roofing board.
　
1997   K. L. Chemicals Co., Ltd. was certified by ISO-9002:1994.
1998 K. L. Chemicals Co., Ltd. successfully developed the phenolic resin for firebrick used in refractory industry. Also cooperated with Industrial Technology Research Institute, together developed the PF forming resin.
　
1999
Continuously exchanging technologies with Dongguan, Suzhou, Tianjin and Thailand resin coated sand manufacturers.
　
2001  Chin Ching Silica Sand Co., Ltd. was certified by ISO-9001:2000.
2002
K.L Chemicals Co., Ltd. and Chin Ching Silica Sand Co., Ltd. successfully developed the hardener and filler for EMC used in IC encapsulation.
　
2003
K. L. Chemicals Co., Ltd. was certified by ISO-9001:2000. and successfully developed the phenolic resin for painting.
　
2004 K. L. Chemicals Co., Ltd. successfully developed Low Hexamine phenolic resin.
　
2006  Taipei main office moved to Sung Kiang Rd., Taipei.
