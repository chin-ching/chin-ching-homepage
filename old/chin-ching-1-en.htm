Starting from selecting the right mine with a high purity of silica, then through the rigid production control, we are able to produce many specs of quality foundry and resin coated sand that have low clay content.
　 　
Foundry sand : Offer in various grain sizes, It is the base material for making CO2, furan, green sand, and resin coated sand molds.
　 Suitable usages:steel, stainless steel, iron, copper and aluminum castings
　 　
Resin coated sand : With rigid quality control, it comes in extensive specs for using in making auto, motorcycle parts. It is also used in pipe, steel, stainless steel, copper, aluminum castings and medium or large iron or alloy castings for making cores and outer molds.
