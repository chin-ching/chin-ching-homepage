Since 1977 when our company was established, we were motivated by wanting to be the best supplier of silica sand with a desire to be constantly improving, developing, and innovating for our products. Over thirty years, we have obtained the supports and acknowledgement from our numerous customers. In silica sand related product markets, Chin Ching always means the best brand that guarantees the best quality
The existing domestic and foreign customers reach more than 600; they are widespread in casting, chemical, construction, glass, refractory material, kiln, fiber glass, and auto parts industries. No matter they are small to medium-sized enterprises or large-scale group companies, once they become our customers, we then form long-term cooperative relationships between us. Starting from supplier evaluations, sample testing, to product applications, and even to after-sale service, we have always been keeping the closest relation with our customers at all time, because we truly believe only the customer's satisfaction could continue to create the win-win situation for both of us.

  Chin Ching Touwu Plant：We have provided our entire facility and latest technologies to Minexco (Vietnam), starting from surveying, mining, washing, classifying to shipping via vessel, each every process has gone through a stringent control system. The high purity Vietnam Camranh Bay silica sand that we distribute, also goes through rigid iron stripping and grain sizing before supplying to glass, foundry, and construction industries. Finally, by using the above silica sand as the main base material, ground by the pebble millers, which are lined with silex, we are able to produce the high purity silica powder with low iron content.
