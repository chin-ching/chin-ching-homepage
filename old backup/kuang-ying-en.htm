Geology & Terrains
All our mines are part of Nanchuan layer of tertiary period in geology, which are considered the best sand layers in Taiwan. Not only those deposits are extremely abundant, they also have good hardness and high SiO2 contain. They are quite suitable for glass and foundry industries, Each mine is excavated in an open air and the total monthly production can be up to 30,000 tons.
