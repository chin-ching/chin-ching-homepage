Starting from selecting the right mine with a high purity of silica, then through the rigid production control, we are able to produce many specs of quality foundry and resin coated sand that have low clay content. Meanwhile we have imported the most advanced thermal reclaiming equipments for recycle sand to meet the requirements of environmental protection.
　
Foundry sand : Offer in various grain sizes, It is the base material for making CO2, furan, green sand, and resin coated sand molds.
　 　
Suitable usages: steel, stainless steel, iron, copper and aluminum castings
　 　
Resin coated sand : With rigid quality control, it comes in extensive specs for using in making auto, motorcycle parts, It is also used in pipe, steel, stainless steel, copper, aluminum castings for making cores and outer molds.
　 　
Reclaiming sand : Those resin coated sand produced by using recycle sand has characteristics such as good thermal shock resistance, reduced fume and odor, and resistance to peel back, Those types of castings have excellent surface and high definition.
